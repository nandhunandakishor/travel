Meenmutty Waterfall

Meenmutty Waterfall is located in Padinjarathara, Wayanad District, Kerala.

It is the largest waterfall in Wayanad, cascading down in three tiers with a height of 1000 feet, located on the scenic Banasura Hills.

Accessible from Padinjarathara, this breathtaking waterfall becomes even more captivating amidst lush green tea plantations.

However, it can be dangerous during the rainy season due to high inflow, and caution is advised as there have been accidents since 1991.

For adventurous tourists, the hike through the green landscape to the falls is a memorable experience.
