S.M. Street, short for Sweetmeat Street, also known as Mittai Theruvu, is a bustling shopping street located in Kozhikode, Kerala.

This pedestrian-only street offers a unique mix of tradition and modernity, with a rich history and a 160-year-old fire temple amidst the shops.

The name derives from the time when the street was lined with sweetmeat and halvah stalls.

Today, it's a vibrant shopping destination, attracting locals and tourists alike.
