Anamudi ("Elephant Head")

Located in Ernakulam and Idukki districts of Kerala, Anamudi is the highest peak in the Western Ghats and South India.

Standing at an elevation of 2,695 metres (8,842 ft) with a prominence of 2,479 metres (8,133 ft),

it offers breathtaking views and is a remarkable symbol of natural beauty.
